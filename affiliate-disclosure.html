Introduction

First in Dallas is committed to transparency and maintaining the trust of our readers, advertisers, and brand partners. This Affiliate Disclosure explains our relationship with third-party businesses, sponsors, and affiliate programs, and how we may earn compensation through certain links, promotions, and partnerships featured in our newsletter and on our website.

FTC Compliance

In accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's (FTC) guidelines concerning the use of endorsements and testimonials in advertising (16 CFR Part 255), we are required to disclose any material connection between First in Dallas and the businesses, products, or services we feature. This disclosure ensures that our readers are fully informed about potential financial relationships that may exist.

Affiliate Relationships and Compensation

First in Dallas may earn commissions, fees, or other forms of compensation through the following means:

Affiliate Links

Our newsletter and website may contain affiliate links to third-party products, services, or websites. When you click on these links and make a purchase or take a qualifying action, we may receive a commission or referral fee from the merchant. This comes at no additional cost to you.

Sponsored Content

We partner with local businesses, brands, and advertisers to feature sponsored content in our newsletter. Sponsored content is clearly labeled and may include paid advertisements, promotional placements, or branded partnerships. We receive compensation for featuring these sponsors.

Direct Partnerships

First in Dallas may enter into direct partnership agreements with businesses, event organizers, real estate professionals, and other entities. These partnerships may involve financial compensation, in-kind services, or other benefits in exchange for promotion, exposure, or advertising space.

Promotional Deals and Offers

When we feature exclusive deals, discounts, or promotional offers from local businesses, we may receive compensation or a commission based on the number of redemptions, clicks, or conversions generated through our platform.

Editorial Independence

While First in Dallas may earn compensation through affiliate relationships and sponsored content, we maintain strict editorial independence. Our recommendations, reviews, and featured content are based on our genuine assessment of value to our Dallas community. We prioritize quality, relevance, and authenticity over financial incentives.

Sponsored content does not influence our editorial decisions regarding non-sponsored content. We clearly distinguish between paid promotions and organic editorial content to ensure transparency for our readers.

Transparency and Labeling

To maintain transparency, we clearly label sponsored content, affiliate links, and paid promotions in our newsletter and on our website. You may see the following disclosures:

  • "Sponsored" or "Paid Partnership" – Indicates content that is financially supported by an advertiser
  • "Affiliate Link" – Indicates a link that may generate commission if you make a purchase
  • "Brought to you by [Sponsor]" – Indicates a brand partner supporting our newsletter
  • "Featured Promotion" – Indicates a paid promotional placement

No Endorsement Guarantee

The inclusion of affiliate links, sponsored content, or promotional offers in our newsletter does not constitute a guarantee, warranty, or endorsement of the quality, safety, or suitability of the featured products, services, or businesses. We encourage readers to conduct their own research and exercise independent judgment before making purchasing decisions.

Reader Trust and Integrity

Building and maintaining trust with our readers is our top priority. We are selective about the businesses and brands we partner with, and we strive to feature only those that align with our values and provide genuine value to the Dallas community. We do not promote products or services that we believe to be misleading, harmful, or of poor quality.
